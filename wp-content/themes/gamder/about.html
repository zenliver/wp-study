About ARTDNA
10 years' experience
Factory 20000 ㎡
More than 40 countries
International certification

Zhongshan Gamder Electrical Appliance Co., Ltd which specializes in developing and producing switches and sockets for 10 years, is an excellent representative of Chinese electrical equipment industry. The factory covers an area of 20,000 square meters, with first-class standard garden style factory buildings which is equipped with excellent design software and advanced international forming and processing equipments, like high precision CNC edm machine, automatic CNC injection molding machine, automatic high-speed punch press and silver point automatic riveting machine…

Being an advocate in the field of electrical industry, Gamder have powerful R&D and manufacturing ability to Self-renewal and improvement continuously. Our production strictly follows the advanced international standards of ISO and IEC. Gamder electrical products not only can fully adapt to the sales of domestic market, but also have the ability to regulate switches and sockets from all regions of the world, including American style, British style German style, Italian style Brazilian style and so on. The products cover more than 40 countries and regions in the world, which have gained the related international products certifications of BS, CE, CB, SASO... We are also the first company to get the Low-Carbon products certification which sued by China Certification Center.

Germany, Japan and China are known as the contemporary world’s three dominant manufacturing countries. “Made in China” has shown its significant competitive advantages around the world. Outstanding Chinese companies are not content to merely export products to the world, they also start to export technology and brands to the world. Based on this concept, Gamder roots the internationalization strategy in the brand essence, and tries to get excellent quality of “Made in China” supported by every family all over the world. Currently we have built up mutually beneficial relationship with ARTDNA brand agencies and OEM customers in more than 25 countries or regions. And it’s going to be more in future. We sincerely hope you will be one of them!
